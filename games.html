Our games:

Geo

Every person knows their shapes and colors, but don't be fooled into thinking this game is a simple matching game. In Geo, as soon as you're getting comfortable, things change in a flash. Can you top the leaderboards?

Color Cross

Hop your way to victory in Color Cross; a constantly changing retro platformer! The environment changes with every jump. So you better hop to it!

Boost

You must survive waves of enemies by using your weapon, which doubles as your sole mode of propulsion. After surviving each wave, you can choose from a variety of unique weapons and powerups.

Happy Town

Create wonderful little towns in this isometric puzzle game! You must place various buildings on floating islands, in a way that everyone is as happy as they can be! It's up to you to figure out where they all go!
